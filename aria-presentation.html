Getting Started

Activate Aria in Seconds

Just say the wake words in your AI IDE. No scripts, no setup, no complexity.

Step 1: Configure Environment

Set up your .env file with Azure credentials and QA settings:

AZURE_ORG="your-org" AZURE_PROJECT="your-project" AZURE_PAT="your-pat-token" QA_ENABLE_CHROME_MCP="true"

Step 2: Wake Up Aria

Use any of these wake phrases in your AI IDE:

"Hey Aria, good morning" "Hey Aria, wake up" "Aria, triage my tickets" "Aria, fix ticket #123456"

Aria reads menthra-aria-agents/ARIA-AUTONOMOUS.md and executes the entire pipeline autonomously.
